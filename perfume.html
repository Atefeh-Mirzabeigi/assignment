Perfume

Directed by: Tom Tykwer

With his incredible talent for discerning scents, Jean-Baptiste Grenouille (Ben Whishaw) is one of 18th-century France's finest perfumers.
He becomes obsessed with capturing an elusive aroma: the scent of young womanhood.
His search takes a deadly turn, and when the bodies of 12 young females are found, panic breaks out, with families rushing to lock up their daughters.
